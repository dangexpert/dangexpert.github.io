Flight Arrival Delay Prediction

United States has some of the largest and busiest airlines in the world in terms of number of destinations served, such as American Airlines, Delta Airlines, and so forth. But when it comes to domestic flights, in recent years, there have been numerous instances of domestic flight delays and cancellations in the US. This study aims at analyzing flight information of US domestic flights operated by 18 airlines, and predicting possible arrival delay of the flight using various machine learning approaches. The Logistic Regression Model is deployed and achieve a maximum accuracy of 62%.
